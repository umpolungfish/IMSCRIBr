IMSCRIBr — Initial Commit
========================

What this is
------------
IMSCRIBr is the standalone iterator for the IMASM space: the enumeration,
classification, and structural fingerprinting of all 429,981,696 arrangements
of 12 tokens across 8 positions (12^8).

A single arrangement is one configuration of the alphabet ⟨A C D E G I L M N O S T V⟩
written into 8 slots.  The combinatorics are immense, but the space collapses
dramatically under the signature algebra — from 430 million down to 165 family
signatures, and from there to exactly 12 canonical classes.

The 12 tokens
-------------
Each token belongs to one of 4 families:

  SACRED (S, A, C)    — the structural triad
  DARK   (D, L, M)    — the latent / shadow operators
  LIGHT  (G, I, T)    — the manifest / radiating operators
  EARTH  (E, N, O, V) — the grounding / material operators

The 8 positions
---------------
Position 0 is always A (origin anchor).  Positions 1–7 are free.
This yields 12^8 = 429,981,696 possible strings.

The 12 canonical classes
-------------------------
Every arrangement reduces, via its coarse structural key, to one of these:

  Class              Key pattern       Signature family
  -----              ----------        ----------------
  1  The Still       A...............   single-token
  2  The Oscillator  A-_-_-_-_-_-_-_   alternating/dyad
  3  The Threshold   A__...........    gate/two-phase
  4  The Vessel      A[....].......    container pattern
  5  The Furnace     A__[....].....    vessel with gate
  6  The Mirror      A...|...|...      reflective/symmetry
  7  The Loom        A:.:.:.:.:.:.:    woven/interleaved
  8  The Churn       A~~^~~^~~^~~^    turbulent/cyclic
  9  The Lens        A><><><><><><>    focusing/directional
  10 The Web         A+_+_+_+_+_+_+    network/mesh
  11 The Pulse       A~...~...~...     rhythmic/dispersed
  12 The Flame       A*..*..*..*..*    ignition/catalytic

  engine.py         — Signature-decomposed enumeration, SpaceMap, search
