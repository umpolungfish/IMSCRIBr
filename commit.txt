ROTAT: the rotation op-opcode over arrangements

tokens.py gains rotat(arr, k) and rotat_orbit(arr). ROTAT is an operator on a
whole arrangement, not one of the twelve tokens. The family signature is
ROTAT-invariant (rotation permutes positions, never the multiset), so an
arrangement and all its rotations share a fingerprint: the rotation orbit is the
equivalence the twelve canonical classes are already read up to.
